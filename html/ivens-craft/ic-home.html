Lead Image Details

Lead image caption: Anonymous, “William Ivens Craft in Coal Yard” (Photograph, 1855-1926), Martin Copeland Private Collection. Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International

Lead image commentary: This photograph, from around 1910, shows William Ivens Craft standing in a coal yard in England with his fellow workers. He is on the viewer's left, leaning against the cart. Though an educated man, as the son of formerly enslaved people, William Ivens struggled to support himself both in the US and in his home country of England. Nonetheless, he managed to carve out a sense of belonging, community, and family that his descendants appreciate to this day.
