Note Bin Habib

  • 20 days from Ja to
      Kiñkonza = 200
  • Nyama kundu bad
  • Kinkonza S of Ujiji –
  •   SW by W to fountains –
  • Lobish comes from
    SW to NE 1 day to N
    from Mpweto's
  • W & by N to stone houses 3 days
  • W to Lufira 1 day         Lufira
