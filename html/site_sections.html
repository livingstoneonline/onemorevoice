Lead Image Details

Lead image caption: Jonathan Adagogo Green, “[Forest Scene]” (Photograph, Nigeria, [1895-1905]), Af,A46.89, British Museum, London. Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International.

Lead image commentary: Jonathan Adagogo Green (1873-1905) was a Nigerian photographer who was active in the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries. His photographs – in contrast to many British photographs produced during the period – offer an indigenous African perspective on local society, landscapes, and the imposition of colonialism in West Africa. One More Voice also publishes a few more photographs from Green and an image of his wetstamp.
