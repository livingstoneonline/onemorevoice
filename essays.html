Lead Image Details

Lead image caption: Yoshio Markino, “Little Venice,” in Oxford from Within, by Hugh De Sélincourt, illustrated by Yoshio Markino (Philadelphia: George W. Jacobs & Co., 1910), opposite 8. Public domain.

Lead image commentary: One More Voice places a significant emphasis on textual production, especially for individuals like Japanese author Yoshio Markino, whose output includes several book-length works. However, as Kaylen Michaelis underscores in a project affiliated with One More Voice, Markino was also an accomplished painter. This image, therefore, speaks to that other aspect of his identity and is one of many, many such visual works that Markino included in his books.
