Lead Image Details

Lead image caption: Anonymous [London Missionary Society Editors], “General Summary to December, 1874,” The Eighty-First Report of the London Missionary Society, 1875, 102. CWML H698. University of London. School of Oriental and African Studies, London. Public domain.

Lead image commentary: This table shows the numerical component of missionary periodicals. It documents data related to numbers of missionaries (British and other), schools, and local (financial) contributions. Similar statements of accounts regularly appear in missionary periodicals from the era, separated into their own sections. Presenting this quantitative data evidences the importance of quantification for missionary periodical audiences and editors. This table provides a visual representation of the language of quantification in missionary periodicals that dehumanizes African Indigenous peoples. Most importantly, the individuals in this table are quantified and correlated to monetary values, further supporting the language of devaluation that is thematically significant throughout many missionary periodicals.
