Lead Image Details

Lead image caption: Anonymous. “The Christian Mother’s Resolution,” Missionary Magazine and Chronicle 13, no. 153 (February 1849): 1, also see 18. CWML G455. University of London. School of Oriental and African Studies, London. Public domain.

Lead image commentary: This image (1) relates to a “brief statement from the memoir of a female convert, written by herself” (18, emphasis in original), one of the periodical pieces identified and documented by the BIPOC Voices project (see Anonymous and Anonymous, “Cuddahap. The Christian Mother,” Missionary Magazine and Chronicle 13, no. 153 [February 1849]: 18). Both the image and narrative gesture towards the real-life, autonomous activities of an older Indian woman. However, the focus on the Christian dimensions of such activities – in a missionary periodical – raises questions about the level of British mediation involved in recording the activities. The mediation, in turn, points to the interpretive complexities raised by the present project and underscores the need for a multicultural set contributors who are appropriately prepared to address such complexities.
