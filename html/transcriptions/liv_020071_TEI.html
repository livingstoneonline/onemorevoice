0001

    12 Cambridge Road C30/10
                Hammersmith
                        March 18/63


Sir/
        I fear you have for-
gotten to send me the paper
for this month.


Please send it by reply


I am Sir
            Yours truly
                                E Craft


to the Clarke



replied to
    20/3/63
