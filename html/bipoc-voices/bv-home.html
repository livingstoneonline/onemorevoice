Lead image commentary: The “BIPOC Voices” project draws attention to the wide range of global creators whose works and words – however problematic – appear in Victorian-era missionary periodicals such as those pictured here. These elements demonstrate that the periodicals are part of a shared global heritage and that key BIPOC-related elements of the periodicals should be made available to as wide an audience as possible, a foundational outcome that has inspired the project as a whole.
